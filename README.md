Food Waste Management System

A web-based platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting individuals, restaurants, and organizations with surplus food to nearby charities, NGOs, and people in need. The system enables real-time food donation listing, location-based matching, and donation tracking to ensure edible food is not wasted.

Problem Statement

A significant amount of food is wasted daily due to lack of proper redistribution channels, while many people still face food insecurity. This system bridges that gap by creating a simple and efficient digital platform for food donation and collection.

Key Features
User registration for donors and receivers (NGOs/individuals)
Add and manage surplus food listings with quantity, expiry time, and location
Location-based matching of available food donations
Request and accept donation workflow
Status tracking for each donation (available, requested, picked up, completed)
Expiry-aware listing to ensure food safety
Basic notification/alert mechanism for new nearby donations (if implemented or future scope)

System Workflow
Donor logs in and adds surplus food details
System stores food listing with location and expiry time
Nearby receivers browse available food listings
Receiver sends request for donation
Donor approves request
Receiver collects food and updates status
System marks donation as completed
